PerSemID View University MSc

This is the view for the university MSc. It offers functionality for opening and inspecting the dossier prepared by the student for the application to masterstudies. Based on the dossier (and possibly information from other sources), the university MSc will decide to either accept or deny the student for the enrollment. This decision is then recorded and the student granted permissions to retrieve it.

A zipped export of the decision is accessible at /hmsc/export/ for authorized users.
